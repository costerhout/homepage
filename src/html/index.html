"The Tao of Raven:" Hayes' new book explores the wisdom of traditional stories

Juneau Empire - Ernestine Hayes used to tell people she didn’t understand the story of Raven and the box of daylight until she had grandchildren. But when one of her grandchildren stole from her, she began thinking about the story in a new way — that of the grandfather and mother “left behind in the dark.” Hayes, a Juneau writer and University of Alaska Southeast assistant professor of English, began to see even more lessons on how to live, how to share and how to persevere. ...More

Your voice is important!

University of Alaska President Jim Johnsen invites your feedback on a proposal in response to current challenges to the UA budget. Strategic Pathways is a framework for a leaner and more streamlined university system on the UA Statewide website. Chancellor Rick Caulfield strongly encourages staff, faculty and students to review the document.  Strategic Pathways will evolve over the next several months, and your input on the proposed focus areas for UAS will be critical to the final outcome.  Email President Johnsen and the Board of Regents to let them know of your support for a vibrant university in Southeast Alaska at ua-pathways@alaska.edu.
